Compu-Torque System Coupling/Tool Bucking unit

This unique state-of-the art system utilizes a high speed computer and interface system to monitor, control and record the spin-up/buck-up process for all types of threaded joints.

The operator inputs joint data, make-up specifications and limits, thereafter, the computer provides high-speed monitoring of conditions as the joint is spun up and bucked up; shuts off the machine within the given parameters; and verifies compliance with and/or deviations from the specifications.
